Initial review lens

Initial manuscript review

The manuscript is strongest when it refuses shortcuts. Ethan is not redeemed by avoiding consequence, and David Matthews is not required to make grief tidy. Grace enters truthfully and then stays.

  • Do not let grace become excuse.
  • Do not let consequence become hopelessness.
  • Keep repentance visible, concrete, and Christ-centered.
  • Let family wounds be handled honestly, not neatly.
